Sentient Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to building open-source AGI, independent of single-entity control.

WHO WE EXIST FOR?

Researchers seeking scientific freedom and credibility.

Engineers who want to build AGI infrastructure in the open.

Artists and thinkers exploring consciousness, intelligence, and human–machine co-creation.

Universities, labs, and independent collectives that believe knowledge should not be gatekept.

OUR BELIEFS

We believe intelligence should be shared, not owned

We believe AGI must be built in daylight, not behind closed doors

We believe that open science, debate, and doubt create safer intelligence than secrecy
