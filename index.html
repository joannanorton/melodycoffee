Symphony of the Soil

Every bean begins with a silent rhythm. Deep in the highlands of Gayo, Sumatra, mineral-rich volcanic soil conducts a masterpiece of nutrients, where tropical microclimates and ancient canopies orchestrate the perfect setting for coffee cherry development.

Volcanic Ash Black Honey Bergamot

The elevation, averaging 1,550 meters above sea level, provides a cool canopy that slows down cherry maturation. This patient ripening process allows sugars to concentrate deeply within the seed, yielding complex floral undertones and a bright, crisp acidity that is unique to our terroir.
